About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an exhilarating survival game that challenges players to endure against relentless waves of monstrous enemies. Set in a beautifully crafted fantasy world, you'll control a heroic character with unique abilities, collecting power-ups and evolving your skills to withstand the increasingly difficult hordes of creatures that come your way.

With its addictive gameplay loop and satisfying progression system, Monster Survivors delivers a perfect balance of challenge and reward. Each session is different, offering nearly endless replayability as you discover new combinations of abilities and strategies.

How to Play

Controls

Getting started with Monster Survivors is simple:

  • Use WASD or Arrow Keys to move your character
  • Your character attacks nearby enemies automatically
  • Collect gems to gain experience points
  • Level up to choose new abilities and power-ups

Strategy Tips

Master these techniques to survive longer:

  • Stay on the move to avoid being surrounded
  • Prioritize picking up experience gems
  • Choose complementary abilities that create powerful synergies
  • Pay attention to your surroundings and plan escape routes
